Beginner Recommendation

For a beginner guitar I highly recommend that you go with something by the Squire line. This is Fender's budget line of guitars, and if you go for a higher quality guitar from this line you won't be disappointed. Personally, I would recommend the Contemporary series, and more specifically the Jaguar From this line. It is a very comfortable body to hold, with a equally comfortable neck. The hardtail design means that you don't have to deal with a tremolo system which can be a headach for new players to get used to. The two humbucker pickups sound very great and will give you a ton of tone options starting out.
